Healthy Habits

Regular Exercise

Regular physical activity, whether it be walking, running, swimming, or another activity, helps to control weight, build strength, and enhance mood.

Balanced Nutrition

Eat a wide range of nutrient-dense foods, such as fresh produce, whole grains, lean meats, and healthy fats. Limit your intake of processed, high-fat, and sugary foods.

Adequate Hydration

Maintain optimum hydration throughout the day, consume enough water. Circulation, controlling body temperature, and other biological processes all require water.

Adequate Sleep

Give yourself 7-9 hours of good sleep each night a priority. For retention of memories, physical and mental healing, and general health, sleep is essential.

Limit Screen Time

To preserve good vision and posture, limit your use of computers and other electronic devices. Maintain a healthy balance between screen time and movement and social engagement.

Stress Management

To control and lower stress levels, use stress-reduction practices like deep breathing, meditation, concentration, or taking up a hobby.
